About Me

Data leader. Engineer.
Builder of systems that scale.

I'm a Senior Data Manager and Analytics Leader based in Kampala, Uganda, with over 8 years of experience building data infrastructure and insight systems that help organisations make faster, smarter decisions.

My work spans the full data value chain — from 1.leading the design of scalable data collection tools. -> to 2. developing both local and cloud-based ETL pipelines in Azure, that check data quality and preserve data integrity. -> to 3 writing SQL querries, Python scripts, and triggers to automate, triangilate and harmonise messy, multi-context datasets. -> to 4. building data visialisation dashboards that lead boardroom discussions and shape organisation decisions.

I've led data strategy for Opportunity International across African markets, managed analytics for 10,000+ foot soldiers/ field agents at Wave Mobile Money, and under Direct Access, led data operations, contributing to Nivea's doubling of its shelf-share/market-share in key supermarkets in Uganda

I believe the mark of great data work isn't technical complexity — it's whether someone made a better decision because of it.
